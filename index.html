About Us

Who are We?

A youth driven non-profit organization committed to turning environmental challenges into opportunities for sustainable development. By integrating principles of sustainability and innovation, we focus on creating eco-friendly products with significant economic value, such as transforming invasive plants like water hyacinth into eco-friendly paper, handicrafts, and other valuable products

Our mission goes beyond environmental preservation; we empower communities by providing the skills and resources to turn waste into valuable products. These efforts drive socio-economic change, promote environmental stewardship, and inspire the next generation to build a sustainable future.

Our Commitment to Sustainable Development Goal

At Greenovation, our goals align with the United Nations Sustainable Development Goals (SDGs). We aim to transform environmental challenges into sustainable opportunities that benefit both the planet and its people.

Providing workshops and practical skills for sustainable living.

Empowering communities to create sustainable livelihoods

Transforming wild plants into valuable products

Take action to mitigate environmental damage
