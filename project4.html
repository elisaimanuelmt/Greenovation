Workshop at Petra 3 Junior High School, Surabaya

On September 20th, 2024, the Greenovation Team held a valuable workshop at Petra 3 Junior High School in Surabaya, involving students from various educational levels. This workshop was part of Greenovation's efforts to educate and empower the community through sustainable living programs.

The workshop began with a presentation on environmental themes, energy conservation, and the positive impacts of water hyacinth processing. We provided an in-depth explanation of the importance of protecting the environment and how simple daily actions can contribute to sustainability. The material was designed to give students clear and practical insights into relevant environmental issues.

Next, we conducted games and ice-breaking sessions that were designed to strengthen togetherness while staying connected to the topics discussed. These interactive activities not only made the atmosphere more enjoyable but also ensured that students could absorb the information more easily.

Afterward, we guided the students through the practical process of turning water hyacinth into paper. This process began with collecting the water hyacinth from rivers and water reservoirs, followed by cleaning and boiling. Then, the water hyacinth was cut, blended, and its fibers were extracted to be molded into sheets of paper. Each step of the process was thoroughly explained so that participants could fully grasp the concept and acquire useful practical skills.

With all these activities, we hope that participants can apply the knowledge and skills they gained to develop their own business ideas, ultimately improving their economy and contributing positively to the environment.
