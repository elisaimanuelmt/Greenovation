Module: Making Homemade Paper from Water Hyacinth

Greenovation has created a comprehensive module on water hyacinth processing that is available online. This module offers detailed, step-by-step instructions on how to turn water hyacinth, a plant often viewed as waste, into valuable products like paper and woven crafts. It covers everything from harvesting and cleaning the plant to the final production stages, making it a useful resource for anyone interested in learning sustainable practices.
